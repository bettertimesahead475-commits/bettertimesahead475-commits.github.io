# Affidavit (Form 35)

## What this document is
An affidavit is a written statement of facts that the person making it swears or affirms to be true. It is used as evidence in court proceedings.

## Why this document exists
The affidavit provides a way to present factual information to the court in a formal, sworn format.

## How to fill it out
1. Write the court file number and location at the top.
2. Identify yourself and your relationship to the child.
3. State the facts in numbered paragraphs.
4. Attach any supporting documents as exhibits.
5. Swear or affirm the affidavit in front of a commissioner for taking affidavits.

## Important note
Make sure all information is accurate, as you are swearing or affirming that it is true to the best of your knowledge.
